My Journey

My path into geospatial technology began with a Bachelor’s in Geoinformatics Engineering from NUST, Pakistan, which gave me a solid foundation in spatial analysis and data science. I went on to complete my MSc in Spatial Engineering at the University of Twente, Netherlands, where I deepened my expertise in using advanced geospatial approaches to address urban and environmental challenges. Along the way, I’ve gained professional experience with organizations such as CGI Netherlands, IQLand.ai, GeoTech, and REMAP.ai, and I’ve also delivered more than 80 freelance projects worldwide through platforms like Fiverr and Upwork. Each step has added new skills and perspectives, shaping the way I approach geospatial problems today.
